About Ekya

Ekya Schools (est. 2010) are progressive K-12 schools in Bengaluru with a focus on personalised learning, inquiry, and real-world projects. With multiple campuses and international curricula options, Ekya prepares learners for a global future.

  • 15+ years of educational excellence
  • 5+ campuses across Bangalore
  • Curricula: CBSE, ICSE, IGCSE (varies by campus)

Curriculum & Pedagogy

Our approach blends research-backed pedagogy with experiential learning. Students engage in design thinking, collaborative projects, and personalized learning pathways. We balance academic rigor with arts, sports, and wellbeing.

Early Years

Play-based discovery, Montessori foundations, and social-emotional learning.

Primary & Middle

Inquiry-led lessons, integrated projects, and skill development across subjects.

Senior Years

Curriculum choices (ICSE/IGCSE/CBSE), university counselling, and career guidance.

Frequently Asked Questions

What curricula are offered at Ekya?

Different campuses offer CBSE, ICSE and IGCSE. Please check the specific campus page or contact admissions for exact offerings.

Where are Ekya campuses located?

Ekya has campuses in BTM Layout, JP Nagar, ITPL, Byrathi, and NICE Road (Bengaluru). Visit the campuses page for addresses and contact numbers.

What age groups do you accept?

Ekya accepts children from Early Years (ages 3+) through Grade 12. Specific intake ages vary by program and campus.

How can parents apply?

Admissions details and application forms are available on the official Ekya contact page. We recommend scheduling a campus visit or counselling session.

Does Ekya offer extracurricular activities?

Yes — arts, music, sports, design thinking labs and community programs are part of the holistic curriculum.
