About Ekya

Ekya Schools (est. 2010) are progressive K-12 schools in Bengaluru with a focus on personalised learning, inquiry, and real-world projects. With multiple campuses and international curricula options, Ekya prepares learners for a global future.

  • 15+ years of educational excellence
  • 5+ campuses across Bangalore
  • Curricula: CBSE, ICSE, IGCSE (varies by campus)

Curriculum & Pedagogy

Our approach blends research-backed pedagogy with experiential learning. Students engage in design thinking, collaborative projects, and personalized learning pathways. We balance academic rigor with arts, sports, and wellbeing.

Early Years

Play-based discovery, Montessori foundations, and social-emotional learning.

Primary & Middle

Inquiry-led lessons, integrated projects, and skill development across subjects.

Senior Years

Curriculum choices (ICSE/IGCSE/CBSE), university counselling, and career guidance.
